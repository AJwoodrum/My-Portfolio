About Me

I went to Cascade high school and started work at an early age, my first real job was at a gourmet dogfood factory at the age of fourteen. Three years later I found a new line of work at a lumber mill called 'Freres'. There I spent a long and physically taxing five years doing six eight hour days, and with plenty of close calls. However after a disagreement with management I decided it best to leave and find a less dangerous job. I then started working for Coca-Cola for three years.

Luckily from my experience at Freres the physical labor was no issue whatsoever. However the hours were unpredictable and fellow coworkers were not great teamplayers. Once the Covid-19 pandemic hit I decided it best to stay home rather than risk the virus from my distributing job, a decision my employers didn't share and considered it volountary leave rather than termination. It was then I decided if physical labor, mental stress and no time with my family was what I really wanted to do for the rest of my life... That was when I heard of Epicodus!

I was both nervous and excited all at the same time, I always wanted to program but haven't even touched a computer for well over a decade. As soon as I started my classes I was pleasantly surprised by the NON-hostile enviroment and fantastic chemistry between my instructors and fellow students! It can be a bit complicated at times but I've always prided myself on my endurableness, and I feel that I'm catching on to the lessons fairly well. I'm VERY excited to learn more into coding and am looking forward to what the future holds.
